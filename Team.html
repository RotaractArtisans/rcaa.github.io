Few Words Charter President , President and IPP

 

Rotaract Club of Ashoka Artisans is extremely close to my heart as I being a Charter President, started this club with a vision to do as much community services as much possible. I got lucky to have a team which was remarkably hardworking and aligned with my vision. We started our Journey with 11 Community-based projects and ran each one of them successfully. I am honored to be part of the club and would continue to devote myself for the continuous growth and development of the club!

Shubhangi Verma

 Charter President ,Social Media Director

I'm honoured to take on the role of the President of this prestigious club, Rotaract Club of Ashoka Artisans. I promise i shall take over the legacy with utmost pride and sincerity.
As President, I will do my very best to listen and ensure transparency.As another chapter begins for 2021-22, there will be many expected and unexpected challenges, ups and downs through out the journey. And I along with the whole family of Ashoka Artisans are ready and willing to face these. Looking forward to a successful, adventurous and satisfying year ahead.I appreciate you taking the time to visit our website and encourage you to join hands with us for a noble cause to serve the community.

Pankaj Arora

 President

Rotaract club of Ashoka Artisans is like a family to me. Each and every member is my family and I truly feels my commitment towards each of them. I feel overwhelmed and so blessed to be a part of such a diverse yet together club. As a president I try  my level best to be committed towards my club and would do whatever it takes to fulfill the aspirations of my club. And I also feel great honoured to be part of Rotary club of Delhi Ashoka who are always there with in all situations supporting and loving us .
