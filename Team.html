Few Words from the Past-President and Current President

 

Rotaract Club of Ashoka Artisans is extremely close to my heart as I being a Charter President, started this club with a vision to do as much community services as much possible. I got lucky to have a team which was remarkably hardworking and aligned with my vision. We started our Journey with 11 Community-based projects and ran each one of them successfully. I am honored to be part of the club and would continue to devote myself for the continuous growth and development of the club

Rtr.Shubhangi Verma 

Immediate Past President

Rotaract club of Ashoka Artisans is like a family to me. Each and every member is my family and I truly feels my commitment towards each of them. I feel overwhelmed and so blessed to be a part of such a diverse yet together club. As a president I try and would keep on trying my level best to be committed towards my club and would do whatever it takes to fulfill the aspirations of my club. And I also feel great honoured to be part of Rotary club of Delhi Ashoka who are always there with in all situations supporting and loving us .

Rtr.Yashika Panwar

President
