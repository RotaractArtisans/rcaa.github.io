About the club

Rotary International is an international service organization whose stated purpose is to bring together business and professional leaders in order to provide humanitarian service and to advance goodwill and peace around the world. It is a non-political and non-religious organization open to all.

Message from the Parent Rotary

 

Rotary Club of Delhi Ashoka is proud to be the parent club of Rotaract Club of Ashoka Artisans. The projects done by the Club are remarkable and are creating an impact by bringing a change step by step. And at each step, we are with them and are supporting them however they require. The young & energetic Ashoka Artisans team is very hard working and dedicated to their work, all driven with a common vision of enhancing community service, selflessly. We are sure they will do wonders and will always have us as their supporters and mentors. coded functions.  

Why Rotaract?

 

Serving the community is not just a Community service project for us but we do actually feel that if each one steps forward to serve the society ,we can together form a new society with a brighter future.

Creative Ideas

Always finding new and creative ways to serve the community within limited resources.

Bigger Networks

Passing Messages to the society
